# Manual Launch Tasks I Cannot Complete For You

Last updated: 26 March 2026

These items require your external accounts, legal decisions, public URLs, or store assets.

## Required manual tasks
1. Choose the public publisher / developer name exactly as it will appear in Google Play.
2. Create or confirm a support email address for Gymlog.
3. Publish the privacy policy at a public, non-editable URL.
4. Add the privacy policy link in Play Console.
5. Add a privacy policy link or privacy text inside the app before public launch.
6. Fill the Google Play Data Safety form in Play Console.
7. Fill App Content declarations in Play Console.
8. Complete the content rating questionnaire in Play Console.
9. Prepare store listing text:
   - app title
   - short description
   - full description
10. Create and upload screenshots.
11. Create and upload the feature graphic.
12. Decide whether your Play developer account is subject to closed-testing requirements and complete them if required.
13. Provide a real upload keystore or decide to use managed signing in your release workflow.
14. Decide whether to disable Android backup or disclose that device-level backup may apply.

## Strongly recommended manual decisions
1. Decide whether AI Coach launches as Beta in the store listing.
2. Decide whether AI Coach stays preview-only in v1 or ships with the live backend enabled.
   - If live backend is enabled, publish the updated privacy policy and re-check Play Data Safety answers.
3. Decide what support promise you want to make publicly, if any.
4. Decide whether to include a public website or landing page.

## Things I can still help you with later
- draft Play Store title, short description, and full description
- draft privacy policy text revisions after your contact details are known
- prepare in-app privacy screen copy
- review screenshots before upload
- review Data Safety answers before you submit them
